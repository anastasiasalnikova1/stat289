Qingdao Metro

Qingdao Metro ( Chinese : 青岛地铁 ; pinyin : Qīngdǎo dìtiě ) is a metro system in the city of Qingdao , Shandong province in China . The first line, Line 3 (north section) began operation on December 16, 2015. [1]
