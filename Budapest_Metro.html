Budapest Metro

The Budapest Metro ( Hungarian : Budapesti metró ) is the rapid transit system in the Hungarian capital Budapest . It is the oldest electrified underground railway system on the European continent , and the third-oldest electrically operated underground railway in the world, predated by the 1890 City & South London Railway (now part of the London Underground ) and the Liverpool Overhead Railway in 1893-96. [6] [7] Its iconic Line 1 was completed in 1896. [8] [9]
