Tbilisi Metro

The Tbilisi Metro ( Georgian : თბილისის მეტროპოლიტენი ) is a rapid transit system in Tbilisi , Georgia . Opened on 11 January 1966, it was the fourth metro system in the former Soviet Union . Like other ex-Soviet metros, most of the stations are very deep and vividly decorated.
