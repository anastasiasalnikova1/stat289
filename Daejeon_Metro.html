Daejeon Metro

Daejeon Metro ( Hangul :  대전도시철도 ; Hanja :  大田都市鐵道 ; RR :  Daejeon dosicheoldo ) is the rapid transit system of Daejeon , South Korea , operated by the Daejeon Metropolitan Express Transit Corporation (DjeT, or Daejeon Metro). The single-line subway network first opened in 2006 with 12 stations. The line was expanded in 2007 and it now consists of one line, 22 operating stations, and 22.6 kilometres (14.0 mi) of route.
