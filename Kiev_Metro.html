The Kiev Metro ( Ukrainian : or ) is a metro system that is the mainstay of Kiev 's public transport . It was the first rapid transit system in Ukraine and the third one built in the USSR (after Moscow and St. Petersburg Metros). It now has three lines with a total length of 63.9 kilometres an 49 stations. [1] The system carries 1.7 to 1.9 million passengers daily [1] , accounting for 34% of the Kiev's public transport load. In 2005, the total number of trips exceeded 600 million. One of the deepest stations in the world, Arsenalna at (105,5 metres) [2] , is found on the system.
