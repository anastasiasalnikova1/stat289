Wuxi Metro

Wuxi Metro is the metro system of Wuxi , Jiangsu province, China . Line 1 began operations on 1 July 2014, [1] and Line 2 on 28 December 2014. [2] [3] Further plans to build 6 more lines and to connect system with the Shanghai Metro and Suzhou Metro are under active review. [4] [5]
