Samara Metro

Samara Metro (Russian: Самарское Метро ), formerly known as the Kuybyshev Metro ( Куйбышевское Метро ), is a rapid transit system which serves the city of Samara, Russia . Opened in 1987, it consists of one line with ten stations [2] and approximately 12.7 kilometres (7.9 mi) of bi-directional track. [1] [2]
