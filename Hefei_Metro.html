Hefei Metro

Hefei Urban Rail Transit ( Chinese : 合肥城市轨道交通 ) abbreviated Hefei Metro ( Chinese : 合肥轨道 ) is a rapid transit system in Hefei , China . Line 1 begun construction on 1 June 2012 and was opened on December 26, 2016. [1] Line 2 opened exactly a year later. [2] [3] [4] [5] Thales Saic Transport is providing the line with urban rail traffic management and CBTC systems.

[1] Line 1 is a 24.6 km long north-south line with 23 stations. [3] Line 2, which is also under construction is scheduled to open in 2017 [4] and be the first east-west subway in Hefei.
