Naples Metro

The Naples Metro ( Italian : Metropolitana di Napoli ; Neapolitan : Metrupulitana 'e Napule ) is the metro system serving the city of Naples , Italy . The system comprises three underground rapid transit lines ( Line 1 , Line 6 and the Naples-Aversa Metro ).

The Naples Metro ( Italian : Metropolitana di Napoli ) is the metro system serving the city of Naples , Italy . The system includes two underground rapid transit lines ( Line 1 and Line 6 ), integrated with some commuter railways (the Line 2 operated by Trenitalia ; Circumflegrea and Cumana operated by SEPSA ; the Naples–Aversa railway operated by MCNE ; and the Circumvesuviana network), with planned upgrading and expansion work underway. The network is complemented by four funiculars (cable railways).
