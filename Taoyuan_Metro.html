Taoyuan Metro

Taoyuan Metro (formerly Taoyuan Rail Transit and officially Taoyuan Mass Rapid Transit System ) is a rapid transit system serving the Taoyuan City area in Taiwan . [2] The proposed network will consist of five lines. The system opened for commercial service on 2 March 2017 with the opening of the Taoyuan Airport MRT .
