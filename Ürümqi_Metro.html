Ürümqi Metro

The Ürümqi Metro or Ürümqi Subway is a metro system in operation in Ürümqi , capital of the Xinjiang Autonomous Region in China . The Ürümqi Metro has submitted plans for 7 lines totalling 211.9 km in length with an estimated cost of 31.24 billion yuan. [1]
