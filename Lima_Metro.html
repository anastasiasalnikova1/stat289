The Lima Metro , locally known as Tren Eléctrico , is an electric mass transit line in the Lima Metropolitan Area , in Peru . It currently consists of one 9.2 kilometres (5.7 mi) line in the southern area of the metropolis that only been opened on a few occasions since its 1997 construction. [1] . Work is under way since March 2010 with public operations hoped for July 2011 [1] .
