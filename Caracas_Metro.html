Caracas Metro

The Caracas Metro ( Spanish : Metro de Caracas ) is a mass rapid transit system serving Caracas , Venezuela . It was constructed and is operated by Compañía Anónima Metro de Caracas, [4] a government-owned company that was founded in 1977 by José González-Lander who headed the project for more than thirty years since the early planning stages in the 1960s. Its motto is "Somos parte de tu vida" (translated as " We are part of your life ").
