Brescia Metro

The Brescia Metro ( Italian : Metropolitana di Brescia ) [5] is a rapid transit network serving Brescia , Lombardy , Italy . The network comprises a single line, having a length of 13.7 kilometres (8.5 mi) and a total of 17 stations from Prealpino to Sant´Eufemia-Buffalora , located respectively at the north and southeast of Brescia.
