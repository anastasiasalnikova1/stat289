Yokohama Municipal Subway ( 横浜市営地下鉄 , Yokohama-shiei chikatetsu ) is the metro network in the city of Yokohama , Japan , in Greater Tokyo . It is operated by Yokohama City Transportation Bureau , and is operated as a single line, though 2 continuous lines exist. Line 4 is under construction and due for opening in March 2008. Future plans exist for 2015 for connection with Odakyu Odawara Line at Shin-Yurigaoka Station , Kawasaki City, and with the future Kawasaki City Subway.
