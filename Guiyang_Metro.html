Guiyang Metro

Guiyang Metro is a metro system in the city of Guiyang , Guizhou province in China operated and branded as Guiyang Urban Rail Transit ( GYURT ). Guiyang Metro was approved for construction on 6 May 2013. Line 1 was opened on 28 December 2017. [1] [2]
