Panama Metro

The Panama Metro ( Spanish : Metro de Panamá ) is a metropolitan rapid transit system in Panama City , Panama , [6] which links Los Andes County with the city center. It was inaugurated on April 5, 2014, and entered revenue service two weeks later. [7]

[2] The Línea Uno consortium, which includes the Spanish Fomento de Construcciones y Contratas (FCC) group, won the contract. [3] The government of Brazil offered to invest in the project.
