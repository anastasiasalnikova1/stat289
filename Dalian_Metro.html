Dalian Metro

The Dalian Metro or Dalian Subway is a rapid transit system in the city of Dalian , Liaoning , China. [2] The metro opened on 1 May 2003. The system currently in operation consists of 4 lines: Line 1, Line 2, Line 3, and Line 12.

Line 3 (cyan line), locally known as Qinggui #3 (confusingly light rail line 3), which connects Dalian Development Zone and Jinshitan with Dalian city center. It is a serpentine shaped line, and is mostly either elevated or at grade. It runs from Dalian Railway Station to Jinshitan. A branch line was added to the line later. This new branch starts at Kāifāqū (开发区) station and stretches Northwest through Jinzhou (金州), ending just short of Pǔlándiàn (普兰店). It runs from Kaifaqu to Jiuli.
