Lisbon Metro

The Lisbon Metro ( Portuguese : Metropolitano de Lisboa ) is the metro (subway) system of Lisbon , Portugal . Opened in December 1959, it was the first subway system in Portugal . As of 2017 [update] , the four Lisbon metro lines total 44.2 kilometres (27.5 mi) of route and serve 56 stations. [1]
